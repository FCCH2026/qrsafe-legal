QR SafeCode — Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 25, 2026

QR SafeCode ("the App") is developed by Privara. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Data We Collect

Analytics (TelemetryDeck)

We use TelemetryDeck, a privacy-focused analytics service, to understand how the App is used. TelemetryDeck collects:

  • Anonymous usage signals (e.g., app launched, QR code scanned, QR code generated)
  • Device model and operating system version
  • App version
  • Country and language (from device settings, not GPS)

This data is never linked to your identity. TelemetryDeck does not use advertising identifiers (IDFA) and does not track users across apps or websites. All user identifiers are hashed before transmission.

Purchase Information

If you subscribe to QR SafeCode Premium, your purchase is processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive or store your payment information, Apple ID, or billing details.

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • No names, email addresses, or contact information
  • No location data (no GPS, no IP-based location tracking)
  • No photos, videos, or files from your device
  • No browsing history or search history
  • No health, fitness, or sensitive personal data
  • No advertising identifiers (IDFA)
  • No third-party tracking or advertising SDKs

3. QR Code Scanning

All QR code scanning and security analysis is performed locally on your device. Scanned QR code content is never sent to our servers. Your scan history is stored only on your device and is never transmitted.

4. Camera Access

The App requires camera access solely for scanning QR codes. Camera data is processed in real-time on your device and is never recorded, stored, or transmitted.

5. Photo Library Access

The App may request access to your photo library to save generated QR codes or to scan QR codes from existing photos. We do not access, upload, or share any other photos from your library.

6. Data Storage

All user preferences, scan history, and app settings are stored locally on your device using secure on-device storage. This data is not backed up to our servers.

7. Third-Party Services

  • TelemetryDeck — Privacy-focused analytics. TelemetryDeck Privacy Policy
  • Apple StoreKit — In-app purchases processed by Apple. Apple Privacy Policy

8. Children's Privacy

The App is rated 4+ and does not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
